Who We Are

Alkor Media School in Thrikkakara, Kochi, Kerala offers a range of courses in film editing, cinematography, sound engineering, graphic design, anchoring, and script writing. With experienced faculty and hands-on training, Alkor Film School is the perfect place to launch your career in the film industry. Enroll today and start your journey towards a successful career in the world of film.

Experienced Faculty

At Alkor Media School, we pride ourselves on our experienced faculty who bring a wealth of knowledge and industry expertise to our students.

Well Equiped Labs

At Alkor Media School, we believe in providing our students with state-of-the-art, well-equipped labs to enhance their learning experience.

Projects
