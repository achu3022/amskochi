Welcome to Alkor Media School

At Alkor Film School, we are dedicated to nurturing creative minds and preparing students for successful careers in the dynamic and ever-evolving world of film and media. Located in the vibrant city of Thrikkakara, Kochi, Kerala, our school offers a wide range of courses designed to equip aspiring filmmakers, editors, cinematographers, sound engineers, graphic designers, anchors, and scriptwriters with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in their chosen fields.

What sets us apart is our commitment to providing a comprehensive learning experience that combines theory with practical, hands-on training. Our experienced faculty members, who are professionals in the industry, bring their expertise and real-world insights to the classroom, ensuring that students receive the most up-to-date and relevant education.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and well-equipped labs provide students with a conducive environment to explore their creativity and develop their technical skills. From editing suites to sound recording studios, we have the tools and resources to foster a dynamic learning environment where students can bring their ideas to life.

At Alkor Film School, we believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork. Through group projects and industry collaborations, students have the opportunity to work alongside their peers and professionals in the field, gaining valuable networking opportunities and hands-on experience in real-world scenarios.

We are proud of our alumni who have gone on to make their mark in the industry, whether it's working on major film productions, creating award-winning short films, or establishing their own successful production companies. Their success is a testament to the quality of education and training they received at Alkor Film School.

Whether you are a passionate beginner or a professional looking to expand your skillset, we invite you to join our community of filmmakers, storytellers, and creative minds. Together, we can unleash your potential and help you carve a successful path in the exciting world of film and media.
